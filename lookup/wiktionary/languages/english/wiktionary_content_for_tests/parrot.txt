===Etymology===
First attested in 1525. From {{uder|en|frm|perrot}}, either a diminutive of {{m|frm|Pierre}} or a shortened form of {{m|frm|perroquet}} (whence also {{m|en|parakeet}}). Compare {{cog|fr|pierrot}} and {{cog|oc|parrat}}. A number of origins have been suggested for {{m|fr|perroquet}}, such as Spanish {{m|es|periquito}} and Italian {{m|it|parrocchetto}}. The relationship between these various words is disputed. Replaced earlier {{m|en|popinjay}}.
