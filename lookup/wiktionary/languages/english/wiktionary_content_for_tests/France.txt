===Etymology===
From {{inh|en|enm|France}}, from {{der|en|fro|France}}, from {{der|en|la|Francia}}, from {{m|la|Francī}}, the name of a Germanic tribe, of unclear (but {{der|en|gem-pro|-}}) origin.<ref>A. C. Murray, ''From Roman to Merovingian Gaul: A Reader''. Broadview Press Ltd, 2000. p. 1.</ref> Believed to be most likely from {{der|en|frk|*Frankō||a Frank}}, from {{der|en|gem-pro|*frankô||javelin}}, ultimately from {{der|en|ine-pro|*preng-||pole, stalk}}. Compare {{m|en|Frank}}.  Displaced native {{ncog|ang|Francland}}.
