==English==

===Etymology 1===
Representing {{m|en|no}} pronounced with the mouth snapped closed at the end. Compare {{m|en|yep}}, {{m|en|welp}}, {{m|en|ope}}, and {{m|en|yup}}.

=====Usage notes=====
The usage as a reply in the form of a single-word sentence has, since the 1850s, been far more common than any others.
